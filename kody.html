My Approach

HR built around your business, not the other way around.

Most small and mid-size companies reach a point where HR becomes a liability instead of an asset. Policies are outdated. Compliance gaps are invisible until they aren't. Managers are making consequential decisions without support. That's the problem I was built to solve.

My approach is embedded and operational. I don't send recommendations from a distance and wish you luck. I work inside your organization alongside your team, learning the business and building HR infrastructure that fits how you actually operate.

That means practical compliance frameworks, systems configured to your workflows, policies your managers will actually follow, and a direct line to senior HR judgment whenever you need it.
